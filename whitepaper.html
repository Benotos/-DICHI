Risk Factors

DICHI is a meme token. Like all meme tokens, it carries significant risks. The following risk factors should be carefully considered before purchasing.

HIGH
Market Volatility — Memecoin prices are highly volatile and can decline by 90%+ in short time periods. DICHI is not immune to this.
HIGH
No Intrinsic Value — $DICHI has no guaranteed utility, revenue, or backing. Its value is entirely driven by community sentiment and speculation.
MEDIUM
Regulatory Risk — Cryptocurrency regulation is evolving globally. Future laws may affect the ability to trade or hold $DICHI in certain jurisdictions.
MEDIUM
Liquidity Risk — While LP is burned, the depth of liquidity may fluctuate. Large sell orders may result in significant price impact.
LOW
Smart Contract Risk — The contract is renounced and cannot be altered. However, undiscovered bugs in the underlying Solana runtime remain a theoretical risk.
